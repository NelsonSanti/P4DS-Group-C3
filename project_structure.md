# Main goal

Understand the geographic distribution of startups in Portugal and explore which socio-economic factors may help explain it.

## Starting question

Why do startups appear to be concentrated in Lisbon and Porto?

## Assumptions

> Startups are likely concentrated in municipalities with higher purchasing power.
>
> Startups are likely concentrated in municipalities with higher levels of education.
>
> Startups are likely concentrated in municipalities receiving more grants and public support.

## Main questions

- How are startups distributed?
- Is there geographic concentration?
- Which municipalities over/underperform?
- Which factors appear associated?

## Ideal Project Structure (Storytelling)

### 1. Assumption
>
> We assumed startups would be heavily concentrated in Lisbon and Porto.

### 2. Data exploration
>
> We analyzed startup distribution and socio-economic patterns across Portuguese municipalities.

### 3. Findings
>
> Some assumptions were confirmed, while others were challenged by the data.

### 4. Recommendations
>
> Based on the findings, we identified potential opportunities and recommendations.
>
> This storytelling approach is extremely important for the final evaluation.
